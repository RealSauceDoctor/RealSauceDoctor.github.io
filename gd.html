Ivan Saucedo's RealSauceDoctor's Game Designer Arc

"(...) The midnight moonlight wrote his name between the shadows of the crooked woods. His name was...

The @RealSauceDoctor...

«What you see here, is my design... my art...» — He said, while his footsteps echoed among the desolated mountains."

Since he was a child, he dreamed of creating his own worlds, but it wasn't until he became a teenager that he heard the call.

At that time he tried to create worlds with tools like GameMaker, RPG Maker, and 3d GameStudio, but all of his attempts perished over time because his knowledge of creating games was very poor.

Giving up, he disappeared for a while, taking other paths such as a musician or a filmmaker would.

However, his dreams never died, and after walking a long way trying to find himself, he became more skilled and more wiser.

He learned to craft better art and tell better stories, which is why, after a Global Game Jam in 2016, he realized it was time to come back.

To return to walk the path of the creator...

The world creator that he always wanted to be.
