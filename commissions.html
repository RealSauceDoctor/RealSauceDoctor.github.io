Commissions

Commissions are now Open!

I am available for commissions! I offer Digital or Traditional, so please choose which is right for you!

Digital Commissions are drawn in Krita and can be delivered as a .jpeg or .png file, or printed and sent to you. Prices range from $50 - $500USD+, depending on complexity.

Traditional Commissions are drawn on 250GSM A4 paper with pencil, oleo, or watercolor. Please keep in mind that Traditional Commissions will only be in black and white. Prices range from $100 - $300USD, depending on complexity. These will be mailed to you!
